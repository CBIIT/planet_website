Additional Resources

Cancer Control P.L.A.N.E.T. provides links to additional resources that offer users information relevant to their cancer control activities. These resources are provided solely as a service to our users.

  • Cancer Trends Progress Report - describes progress and advances in reducing cancer in the U.S. in relation to Healthy People target goals
  • Annual Report to the Nation on the Status of Cancer - provides an annual update on the cancer burden and trends in the U.S.
  • GW Cancer Institute Cancer Control Tap - provides training and technical assistance to the National Comprehensive Cancer Control Program
