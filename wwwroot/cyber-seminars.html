 Cyber-Seminar Series

About This Series

The goal of the P.L.A.N.E.T. Step 2 Cyber-Seminar Series is to bring researchers and cancer control practitioners together to discuss the successes and challenges of translating research into practice. This series is part of a larger effort to build a community of practice on Step 2 of Cancer Control P.L.A.N.E.T., providing a more engaging communication platform for the cancer control community. The webinar series is the first step in building this community, and dynamic features will be added to the site based on the results of user testing and feedback.

Cyber-seminars will cover a variety of topics relevant to the 5 "steps" of Cancer Control P.L.A.N.E.T. including user needs assessment, partnership, Research-tested Intervention Programs (RTIPs), program adaptation, and lessons learned through dissemination. Unless noted, they will be held on the last Tuesday of each month at 2:00 p.m. ET and will be archived on this site a week following the presentation. Audience members will be invited to submit questions live and online and will be asked to answer a few questions OR complete a brief evaluation following the presentation.
