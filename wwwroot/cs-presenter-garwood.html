Kathy Garwood, M.Ed.

Kathy Garwood is the Executive Director of the non-profit that serves as the Family Connection collaborative in Crisp County, Georgia and is Director of a Family Resource Center. She has over 15 years of experience in non-profit management, grant writing, community strategic planning, and strategy level evaluation. She has experience in implementing a variety of programs addressing the non-profit’s mission of improving conditions for families and children such as after-school programs for adolescents, teen pregnancy prevention programs, parent involvement programs, and most recently, supervision of a Parents as Teachers parenting program for seven years. For this mini-grants program, she supervised implementation of the Parents As Teachers - High 5 Low Fat program. Prior work experiences include child protective services and Drug-Free Prevention Coordinator with her local school system. She has a master's degree in School Counseling.
