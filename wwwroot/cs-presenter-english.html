Kevin English, R.Ph., M.P.H.

Kevin English is a Dr.P.H. candidate in the Sociomedical Sciences Department at Columbia University’s Mailman School of Public Health. He leads the implementation of the perinatal tobacco cessation program at Downstate New York Healthy Start. His work centers on translational research aimed towards the amelioration of racial, ethnic, and socioeconomic health disparities. He focuses on a mixed-method approach to explore individual, community, and system level determinants that shape health disparities and integrates a community-based participatory approach into his work.
