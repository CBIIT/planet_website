Clinical Guideline Summaries

This page provides a synopsis of cancer-related recommendations for clinical preventive services based on reviews of evidence of their effectiveness. [Rationale for using US Preventive Services Task Force recommendations]

Follow the links for the full text of the recommendation for each topic. Also available for download to PDA.

Counseling (Prevention):

Tobacco Use
USPSTF Guidelines (1996)
  • Strongly recommend (A): screen for tobacco use and provide tobacco cessation interventions...
 
Diet
USPSTF Guidelines (2003)
  • Insufficient evidence (I): routine behavioral counseling to promote a healthy diet...


  • Recommend (B): intensive behavioral counseling for adult patients with known risk factors...
 
Physical Activity
USPSTF Guidelines (2002)
  • Insufficient evidence (I): behavioral counseling to promote physical activity...
Skin Cancer
USPSTF Guidelines (2003)
  • Insufficient evidence (I): routine counseling to prevent skin cancer...
 

Screening (Detection):

Colorectal Cancer
USPSTF Guidelines (2002)
  • Strongly recommend (A): screen men and women 50 years of age or older for colorectal cancer...
 
Cervical Cancer
USPSTF Guidelines (2003)
  • Strongly recommend (A): screen for cervical cancer in women who meet certain conditions...


  • Recommend against (D): routine screening of women older than age 65 who meet certain conditions...


  • Recommend against (D): routine Pap smear screening in women who have had a total hysterectomy for benign disease...


  • Insufficient evidence (I): routine use of new technologies to screen for cervical cancer...


  • Insufficient evidence (I): routine use of human papillomavirus (HPV) testing as a primary screening test for cervical cancer...
 
Breast Cancer
USPSTF Guidelines (2002)
  • Recommend (B): screening mammography every 1-2 years for women aged 40 and older...


  • Insufficient evidence (I): routine clinical breast examination alone to screen for breast cancer...


  • Insufficient evidence (I): teaching or performing routine breast self-examination...
