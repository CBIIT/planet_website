STEPS for signing-up for the Cancer Control P.L.A.N.E.T. List Serve:
  1. Using your e-mail service - enter listserv@list.nih.gov in the TO: box.


  2. In the body of message content - enter subscribe Cancer_PLANET your name, for example, subscribe Cancer_PLANET Judy Kuan. Make sure there is no additional text in the message body including electronic signatures. No subject line is necessary.


  3. Following your registration you will receive a confirmation e-mail asking you to verify your registration to the list serve Cancer_PLANET.

**To unsubscribe from a list, send an e-mail to listserv@list.nih.gov with the following text in the message body: unsubscribe Cancer_PLANET
