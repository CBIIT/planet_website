Michelle Carvalho, MPH

Michelle Carvalho is the Team Lead for the Cancer Prevention and Control Research Network (CPCRN) of the Emory Prevention Research Center in Atlanta, GA. She has over 16 years of domestic and international community health experience and over 6 years of public health research. She completed her Masters degree in Public Health in Prevention Science at Emory University’s Rollins School of Public Health. Over the past 6 years, she has been working through the CPCRN on the dissemination of evidence-based approaches in national workgroups, local mini-grants programs, training and technical assistance in rural communities in Georgia. Her research interests focus on chronic disease prevention through the promotion of healthy diet, physical activity and early cancer detection.
