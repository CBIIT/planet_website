Del Doss-Hemsley, M.A., D.L.

Del Doss-Hemsley, M.A., D.L., is Director of Health Ministries at Lane Tabernacle CME Church in St. Louis, MO and is a founding member of Faith Communities Joined for Health (FCJH), a regional consortium formed to empower African American churches to lead efforts aimed at enhancing the health of their members and the residents in the communities they serve. Del has more than 25 years experience working in the lay and faith-based community to address health disparities. She has been instrumental in incorporating parish nurses in St. Louis CME District churches, serves on the Colorectal Cancer Advisory Board for the NCI-Designated Siteman Comprehensive Cancer Center Community Networks Program, is active in regional collaborations to provide prostate cancer education, and is currently involved in training local churches to implement Body & Soul. She is a member of the Missouri Comprehensive Cancer Consortium.
