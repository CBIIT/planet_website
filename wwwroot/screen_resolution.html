Changing your screen resolution for best viewing

  1. Click on the Start Menu.
  2. Click Settings.
  3. Click Control Panel.
  4. Double click the icon or word - Display.
  5. Click on the Settings tab.
  6. In the screen area, adjust the slider up/to the right to increase the resolution of your screen. To best view this site, move the slider to at least 1024 by 768 and click "Apply". If you can not move the slider to the right, then your monitor can not handle an increase in screen resolution. You can still use the Web site, but it will be more cumbersome.


  7. After you've done this, most probably a prompt will pop up explaining to you that your settings will now be changed. Click "Ok".


  8. As the computer is adjusting your screen's resolution it will prompt you one last time to keep your current settings. Click "Yes". This will provide you with the best screen resolution to view this site.
