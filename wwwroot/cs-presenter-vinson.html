Cynthia Vinson, M.P.A.

Cynthia Vinson is a Public Health Advisor focusing on dissemination and implementation in the Division of Cancer Control and Population Sciences (DCCPS) at the National Cancer Institute (NCI). In her current position as Dissemination and Implementation Coordinator for the Designing for Dissemination team she works on building and sustaining the field of dissemination and implementation (D&I) science in order to enhance the integration of evidence-based guidelines, programs, and policies for cancer control in public health and clinical practice. She is responsible for working both within NCI and with other agencies and organizations at the international, national, state and local level to translate research funded by DCCPS into practice. In this capacity she has worked to develop and implement the Cancer Control P.L.A.N.E.T. (Plan, Link, Act, Network with Evidence-based Tools) web portal and the Research-tested Interventions Programs (RTIPs) web site in the US. Working collaboratively with the Canadian Partnership Against Cancer she has been able to facilitate the development of a Canadian version of the Cancer Control P.L.A.N.E.T.
