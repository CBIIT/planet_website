Ginny Thompson Kirklin, M.P.H., C.H.E.S.

Ginny Thompson Kirklin, M.P.H., C.H.E.S., joined the Houston Affiliate of Susan G. Komen for the Cure as the Mission Director in February 2010. In her role, she oversees the grant making process for over $3.6 million in community grants, as well as education outreach and public policy efforts. She has over 13 years experience in conducting cancer intervention research, program planning, developing evaluation tools, coalition building and training. She has volunteered for Komen Houston for many years and served as the Education Committee Chair and Chair of Photovoice. Before coming to the Affiliate, Ginny spent 6 years at M. D. Anderson Cancer Center as the Manager for the National Cancer Institute’s Cancer Information Service, serving Texas and Oklahoma. Ginny holds a bachelor’s degree from Texas A&M University and earned her master’s degree from the University of Texas School of Public Health.
