Andrea Caracostis, M.D., M.P.H.

Dr. Andrea Caracostis is the Executive Director of HOPE Clinic, a Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC) Look-Alike whose mission is to provide quality health care without any prejudice to all people of greater Houston, in a culturally and linguistically competent manner. She is dedicated to medically underserved, and as their champion, she is passionate about eliminating disparities through improving health care access.

Dr. Caracostis has a long track record of dedication to special populations and to community health center work. She is a medical doctor with a master's in public health. For 10 years, Dr. Caracostis worked as a provider in a 330 funded Migrant Health Center (MHC), and she had also served as a consultant to Migrant Clinicians Network providing technical assistance to Community Health Centers around the country. She is active in the Bureau of Primary Health Care's Health Disparities Collaboratives and is a member of many national health care advisory committees.

Aside from bounteous knowledge of public health, Dr. Caracostis is multi-lingual, being fluent in Spanish, German, and Portuguese, and she proficient in French. Dr. Caracostis is currently adding Greek to her rich linguistic skills.
