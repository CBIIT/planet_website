Accessibility

We are making every effort to ensure that the information available on the Cancer Control P.L.A.N.E.T. Web site is accessible to all. If you use special adaptive equipment to access the Web and encounter problems when using our site, please let us know. Contact us and we will provide the information to you in an alternate format. It would be helpful if you can be as specific as possible when describing the information you seek.
